Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT)

An interactive deep dive into the core mechanisms, evolution, and practical applications of P-tuning, Prefix-Tuning, Prompt Tuning, and ZeroTuning.

As Large Language Models (LLMs) grow exponentially in size, traditional full fine-tuning has become prohibitively expensive and inefficient. Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) techniques have emerged to address this, aiming to match the performance of full fine-tuning by adjusting only a tiny fraction of the model's parameters. This guide explores several leading prompt-based PEFT methods and their training-free alternatives.
